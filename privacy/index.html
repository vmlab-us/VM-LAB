Website and contact forms

When you use VM LAB websites or contact forms, we may collect information you voluntarily provide, such as your name, email, company, inquiry topic, and message.

We use this information to respond to inquiries, provide support, evaluate business or web project requests, maintain records, prevent spam or abuse, and operate the website.

VM LAB may use analytics tools to understand website traffic and improve user experience. App-specific sections above explain whether each app uses analytics.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information in the traditional sense. We may share information with service providers that help operate the website, process forms, provide analytics, manage email communication, or support business operations.

Security and retention

We use reasonable measures to protect information, but no internet transmission or storage system is completely secure. We keep information as long as reasonably necessary for business, support, legal, or operational purposes.

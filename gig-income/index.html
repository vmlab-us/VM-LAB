GIG Income

Track real gig profit across multiple apps.

GIG Income helps multi-app gig workers record income, expenses, net profit, goals, calendar month and yearly stats, platform comparison, scheduled blocks, and Amazon Flex hours in one simple iPhone app.

Version 3.0.2: Amazon Flex hour forecasts, scheduled blocks, goals, yearly insights, and faster income entry.

Available on the App Store.

Amazon Flex Hours

Built for drivers who use more than one gig app

One platform can show its own payout. GIG Income is for the bigger picture: everything you earn, spend, and actually keep across all your gig work.

Income

Track multiple gig apps

Record income from Amazon Flex, DoorDash, Uber Eats, Instacart, Spark, Grubhub, Lyft, Uber, DeliverThat, and other platforms.

Profit

Know your real costs

Track gas, charging, food, parking, tolls, repairs, supplies, maintenance, and other gig expenses.

Goals

Net income goals

Set goals based on what you actually keep, not just gross platform payouts.

Month and year views

Review calendar month and yearly income instead of relying only on weekly payout screens inside gig apps.

GIG Income Feature

Amazon Flex Hours Tracker

Track rolling hours, estimated hours, and scheduled blocks.

GIG Income helps Amazon Flex drivers keep a clearer picture of Flex work: estimated hours, scheduled blocks, rolling 7-day hours, and remaining available hours.

Available on the App Store.

Gig Economy Guides

Why Amazon Flex hours need separate tracking

Rolling 7-day view

See your estimated Flex hours over a rolling 7-day period instead of trying to calculate everything manually.

Scheduled blocks

Record planned blocks so future hours are visible before the work is completed.

Remaining hours

Estimate how many hours may still be available based on your recent and scheduled Flex activity.

Built for drivers who use Amazon Flex with other apps

Many drivers do not work in Amazon Flex only. They combine Flex blocks with DoorDash, Uber Eats, Instacart, Spark, or other platforms. GIG Income is designed to bring that work into one place so you can understand your full gig-work picture.

Available on the App Store.

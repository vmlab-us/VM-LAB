Frequently Asked Questions

Does GIG Income connect to DoorDash, Uber, or other gig platforms?

No. GIG Income is a manual tracking app. It does not connect to third-party gig-platform accounts and does not ask for gig-platform login credentials.

Where is my data stored?

Income and expense records are stored on your device. If iCloud Sync is available and enabled, data may sync through your private Apple iCloud account.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete entries inside the app where available. You can also remove the app from your device. If iCloud Sync is enabled, related iCloud data may also need to be managed through Apple device or iCloud settings.

How do I restore purchases?

If you purchased Pro features through Apple In-App Purchases, use the Restore Purchases option inside the app when available. Purchases are managed by Apple.

Does GIG Income provide tax advice?

No. GIG Income is not a tax, accounting, legal, or financial advisory service. It is a personal tracking tool.

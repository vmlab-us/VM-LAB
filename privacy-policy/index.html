VM LAB Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 14, 2026

1. Introduction

VM LAB (“VM LAB,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you visit our website, submit an inquiry, request a project estimate, communicate with us, or use our web design, development, consulting, or digital services.

By using the VM LAB website or submitting information to us, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

  • Contact information, such as your name, email address, phone number, company name, website URL, and project-related details.
  • Project inquiry information, including your goals, budget, timeline, platform preferences, branding materials, website needs, business description, and files or links you choose to provide.
  • Communications, including emails, form submissions, messages, feedback, support requests, and related business correspondence.
  • Technical and usage data, such as IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, referring pages, pages visited, time on site, and general analytics information.
  • Cookies and similar technologies used for website functionality, analytics, security, and performance measurement.

3. How We Use Information

  • Respond to inquiries, estimate requests, project questions, and customer communications.
  • Prepare proposals, quotes, project scopes, timelines, and service recommendations.
  • Provide, manage, and improve web design, development, consulting, support, and related digital services.
  • Operate, maintain, secure, and improve the VM LAB website.
  • Analyze website traffic, visitor behavior, service interest, and marketing performance.
  • Send service-related messages, project updates, administrative notices, and, where appropriate, marketing communications.
  • Maintain business records, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

4. Client Materials and Project Information

If you provide logos, brand assets, images, website content, business information, access credentials, documents, or other project materials, we use those materials only for the purpose of evaluating, preparing, delivering, or supporting the requested services, unless you authorize another use.

You are responsible for ensuring that you have the right to provide any materials, content, images, data, or third-party assets submitted to VM LAB.

5. Cookies and Analytics

VM LAB may use cookies, analytics tools, tags, pixels, and similar technologies to understand how visitors use the website, improve functionality, measure campaign performance, and protect the website from abuse. You can control cookies through your browser settings, although disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

6. How We Share Information

We do not sell personal information in the traditional sense. We may share information with:

  • Service providers that help us operate the website, host files, manage forms, provide analytics, process payments, communicate with clients, or support business operations.
  • Project-related contractors or collaborators when necessary to evaluate, deliver, or support requested services.
  • Professional advisors, including attorneys, accountants, consultants, and insurance providers.
  • Government authorities, courts, regulators, or other parties when required by law or necessary to protect rights, safety, and security.
  • Business successors in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, sale of assets, reorganization, or similar transaction.

7. Confidentiality of Client Information

We understand that client project information may be sensitive. We take reasonable steps to keep project communications and client-provided materials confidential and use them only for legitimate business and service purposes. Any additional confidentiality commitments should be documented in a separate written agreement, NDA, proposal, or services contract.

If you contact us, request information, or subscribe to updates, we may send messages related to VM LAB services, project opportunities, updates, or administrative matters. You may unsubscribe from marketing emails using the unsubscribe link or by contacting us. We may still send service-related or transactional messages where necessary.

We retain personal information and project records for as long as reasonably necessary to provide services, respond to inquiries, maintain business records, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Retention periods may vary based on the type of information and the business or legal purpose for keeping it.

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect personal information and client materials. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure. Please avoid submitting highly sensitive information through website forms unless necessary.

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, portability, restriction, or objection regarding your personal information. You may also have the right to opt out of certain sharing, targeted advertising, or marketing communications.

VM LAB Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 16, 2026

1. Introduction

VM LAB (“VM LAB,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you visit our website, submit a contact form, request information, communicate with us, or interact with VM LAB projects, services, websites, and business tools.

By using this website, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the website or submit personal information through it.

2. Information We Collect

  • Contact information, such as your name, email address, company name, inquiry topic, and any details you submit through the contact form.
  • Business inquiry information, including messages related to partnerships, web projects, project-specific requests, or general business communications.
  • Communications, including emails, support requests, feedback, and other information you voluntarily provide.
  • Usage data, such as pages visited, browser type, device information, IP address, referring pages, approximate location, time spent on pages, and similar analytics data.
  • Cookies and similar technologies, including analytics cookies, session cookies, and tools used to improve the website and understand visitor behavior.

3. How We Use Information

  • Respond to business, partnership, project-specific, and web project inquiries.
  • Provide, operate, maintain, and improve the website and related VM LAB project pages.
  • Communicate with visitors, clients, partners, vendors, and project contacts.
  • Evaluate potential collaborations, project requests, service inquiries, and business opportunities.
  • Analyze website performance, traffic, user behavior, and marketing effectiveness.
  • Maintain business records and internal administrative documentation.
  • Prevent spam, fraud, abuse, security incidents, and unauthorized activity.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our rights, policies, and agreements.

4. Contact Forms and Email Communications

When you submit a form on the VM LAB website, we may collect and process the information you provide in order to review and respond to your inquiry. Form submissions may be processed through third-party tools such as Google Apps Script, email services, hosting providers, or other business tools used to operate the website.

If you contact VM LAB by email or through a form, we may use your contact information to respond, follow up, clarify your request, or continue related business communication.

5. Cookies, Analytics, and Website Technologies

VM LAB may use cookies, analytics tools, pixels, log files, and similar technologies to understand website traffic, improve user experience, measure campaign performance, and maintain website functionality.

These technologies may collect information about your device, browser, interactions with the website, and pages visited. You can usually control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some website functionality.

6. How We Share Information

We do not sell personal information in the traditional sense. However, we may share information with:

  • Service providers that help us host the website, process form submissions, manage email communication, provide analytics, store data, or support business operations.
  • Professional advisors, including attorneys, accountants, consultants, contractors, and insurance providers.
  • Technology platforms and tools used for website hosting, forms, analytics, email delivery, project management, or business operations.
  • Government authorities, regulators, courts, or other parties when required by law or necessary to protect rights, safety, and security.
  • Business successors in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, sale of assets, reorganization, or similar business transaction.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, including to respond to inquiries, maintain business records, manage projects, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

Retention periods may vary depending on the type of information and the reason it was collected.

8. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect personal information. However, no website, internet transmission, email, or storage system is completely secure. You use the website and submit information at your own risk.

9. Your Privacy Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, portability, restriction, or objection regarding your personal information. You may also have the right to opt out of certain sharing, targeted advertising, or marketing communications where applicable.

To make a privacy request, contact us through the contact page or by email. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

10. California Privacy Notice

If you are a California resident, California privacy laws may provide additional rights regarding your personal information. These may include the right to know what personal information we collect, use, disclose, or share; the right to request deletion or correction; the right to opt out of certain sharing or targeted advertising; and the right not to be discriminated against for exercising privacy rights.

We do not knowingly sell personal information for money. If our use of analytics, advertising, or website tools is considered “sharing” or a “sale” under applicable California law, you may request to opt out by contacting us.

11. Children’s Privacy

The VM LAB website is not intended for children under 13 years old, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us so we can take appropriate steps.

12. Third-Party Links

The website may link to third-party websites, tools, platforms, project pages, or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or policies of third parties. Review their privacy policies before providing information to them.

13. International Visitors

If you access VM LAB from outside the United States, you understand that your information may be processed and stored in the United States or other locations where our service providers operate. Privacy laws in those locations may differ from those in your country.

14. Updates to This Privacy Policy
